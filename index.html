Visit Luanda

Enjoy culture and fine dining in the vibrant capital of Angola.

You are going to love Luanda!

Once a place of conflict, Luanda has reinvented itself as a peaceful, stable modern city. There has been a surge in the country's fortunes, which have restored a city that was historically a center for trade, culture, and commerce. Today you'll find a chic luxury beach party scene, fine dining, and shopping in a lush and welcoming tropical locale.

Top three activities to do in Luanda

Visit the beaches

Many great beaches to explore around the city, including Sangano Beach just to the south of the city.

Traces of History

Visit the old Portuguese Fortress of São Miguel that overlooks the city from the hill.

Great eats

From fine dining to fabulous street food, and fresh seafood dishes, there is a diverse restaurant scene for your taste buds to explore.
